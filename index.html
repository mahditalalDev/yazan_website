About me

Hey there! I'm Yazan Hammadi, a passionate and creative barista and barman with over eight years of hands-on experience in crafting exceptional beverages—from unique shakes to international cocktails. With a background in restaurant and bar management, I excel at delivering memorable customer experiences and leading vibrant, dynamic teams. Whether I'm behind the bar creating your favorite drink or supervising the floor to keep things running smoothly, my goal is always to bring a touch of creativity and professionalism to everything I do. When I'm not mixing up something special, I enjoy exploring new beverage trends and experimenting with flavors to surprise and delight my guests. I'm excited to bring my expertise in customer service, bar management, and culinary knowledge to any team that values innovation and a welcoming atmosphere. Feel free to check out my CV for a deeper dive into my journey and skills, and let’s connect if you’re looking for someone who can elevate your hospitality experience!
